About College

Established in the year 2002 by dedicated lecturers and social workers, Oxford institution has been marching ahead to stand out the excellent center of learning as a result of committed academics team of distinguished staffs. To quench the thirst of students for university education, Oxford has been successfully running BBS & B.Ed programs & has recently launched BHM & BIM program from Academy year 2075. An adequate educational environment has been set up to impart the quality education to the aspiring students. Oxford believes in quality and practical education which certainly helps to develop confidence and cultivate discipline in the students.
